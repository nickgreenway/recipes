# One-Pot Broccoli Cheddar Soup

### Ingredients (for 8 servings)
- 4 heads broccoli, chopped
- 1 stick butter
- 1 onion, diced
- 1/2 cup flour
- 4 cups half & half
- 4 cups vegetable stock
- 1 cup shredded carrots
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 2 teaspoons pepper
- 1 teaspoon tuscan seasoning
- 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
- 4 cups grated cheddar cheese

---

   Enjoy hot and creamy!
